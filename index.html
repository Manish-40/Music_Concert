About the band

A stage show built on two generations of music

Antra Music is a musical and entertainment band of talented stage performers who've taken the show to cities and towns across India. From comfort of home, you can now follow the tour, see what's next, and book your seat — no matter where in the world you're watching from.

What started as one performer's stage has grown into a full live show, carrying forward a family's musical heritage to new audiences with every city on the route.

On stage

The performers

Founder & lead

Santosh Kapare

Santosh Kapare is not only a musician but a guiding light in the world of music. His passion, dedication, and remarkable talent have left a mark on music lovers across the globe. Through mastery of various musical genres, he's spent a career captivating audiences with soul-stirring compositions and virtuoso performances — paving the way for the next generation to follow.

Vocalist

Shashank S. Kapare

Shashank Kapare is a talented, versatile young singer from Thane, raised inside a family with rich musical heritage. His passion for music blossomed under the guidance of his father, Santosh, and today his repertoire spans classical melodies to contemporary tunes — a remarkable vocal range that's already earning him admiration from audiences and critics alike.
